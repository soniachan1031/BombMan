<p><b>First BombMan Mono Game!</b></p>
<p>This is our first mini game built in C# MonoGame. </p>

<p><b>Key Features:</b></p>
<li><b>Strategic Bomb Placement:</b>  Bombs explode in a cross shape after a short delay, so plan your moves wisely!.</li>
<li><b>Dynamic Levels:</b> Each level is packed with breakable blocks and tricky enemies to challenge you.</li>
<li><b>Power-Ups:</b> Boost your gameplay with speed boosts, extra bombs, and bigger explosion ranges.</li>
<li><b>Survival Mechanics:</b> Dodge enemies and avoid your own bomb explosions to stay in the game.</li>
<li><b>Intuitive Controls:</b> Simple key controls let you move, place bombs, and explore levels with ease.</li>
